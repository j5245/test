Shipping Policy

We ship all orders via FedEx, fully certified and insured. Delivery typically takes 2 to 3 business days. A signature is required upon delivery—either from the recipient or another resident at the same address. If no one is available to sign, the package will be held at your nearest FedEx location for pickup.

Once your order is shipped, you’ll receive a text with your tracking number and a link to monitor your package online.

Note: We do not guarantee delivery times, as delays may occur due to circumstances beyond our control.

We strongly encourage all customers to inspect their packages upon arrival for any signs of damage or tampering.
